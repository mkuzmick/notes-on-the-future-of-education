# BGF Research

Primary sources and faculty resources compiled by the Bok Graduate Fellows.

## Research Papers

- [**Using Oral Exams in Physics and Astronomy Courses**](https://files.slack.com/files-pri/T0HTW3H0V-F0AHZT57EUD/using_oral_exams_in_physics_and_astronomy_courses_-_2509.09846v1.pdf?pub_secret=a4b87ed37b) — Gardner & Giordano (2025). Practical guide to oral exam design in STEM, with question structures based on Bloom's taxonomy.

- [**The Wicked Problem of AI and Assessment**](https://files.slack.com/files-pri/T0HTW3H0V-F0AJ3A4294J/the_wicked_problem_of_ai_and_assessment.pdf?pub_secret=d4dd6bc5ab) — Corbin, Bearman, Boud & Dawson, *Assessment & Evaluation in Higher Education* (2025). Frames AI and assessment as an inherently unsolvable problem requiring iteration, compromise, and context-specific responses.

- [**Oral Exams for a Generative AI World: Managing Concerns and Logistics for Undergraduate Humanities Instruction**](https://files.slack.com/files-pri/T0HTW3H0V-F0AHZT4Q43X/oral_exams_for_a_generative_ai_world_managing_concerns_and_logistics_for_undergraduate_humanities_instruction.pdf?pub_secret=50cf8cb7f0) — Hartmann, *College Teaching* (2025). Implementation case study from the University of Wyoming: logistics, rubric design, student anxiety management, and time comparisons with written assessment.

- [**The Oral Exam — Learning for Mastery and Appreciating It**](https://files.slack.com/files-pri/T0HTW3H0V-F0AHMS6RJAK/jethe-1493-akkaraju.pdf?pub_secret=cddb2f0802) — Akkaraju, *Journal of Effective Teaching in Higher Education* (2023). Scaffolded oral assessment in physiology at CUNY: 74% mastery, 90% proficiency, academic dishonesty "almost irrelevant."

## Faculty Resources on Oral Assessment

- [**Making Assessment Meaningful and Fun: Using Oral Exams**](https://almanac.upenn.edu/articles/making-assessment-meaningful-and-fun-using-oral-exams) — UPenn Almanac. Faculty perspectives on oral exam design and implementation.

- [**Using Oral Exams**](https://mcgraw.princeton.edu/faculty/teaching-princeton/faculty-resource-library/using-oral-exams) — Princeton University McGraw Center for Teaching and Learning. Faculty resource library guide.

- [**Oral Exams as Alternative and Authentic Assessments**](https://blog.uwgb.edu/catl/oral-exams-as-alternative-and-authentic-assessments/) — University of Wisconsin–Green Bay Center for Advancement of Teaching and Learning. Practical overview with pros/cons and implementation tips.
