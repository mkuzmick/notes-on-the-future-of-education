Elle's notes:
Key insight: Elite consulting firms assess candidates almost entirely through oral case interviews. BCG's explicit guidance: "Do: Structure the problem, develop a framework, show business judgment, think before speaking." "Don't: Rush into analysis without understanding the problem, hide your thought process, defend your solution at all costs." The interview simulates "real-world client problems."

BCG Case Interview Preparation

BCG offers case interview preparation resources for candidates applying to client-facing roles. "Case interviews are a key step in the interview process for client-facing roles, simulating real-world client problems."

Interactive Case Quizzes:
1. Climate Strategy — Develop a business case for environmental impact reduction
2. Digital Strategy — Address declining customer satisfaction at a digital bank
3. Cloud Migration — Evaluate enterprise resource planning system upgrades

Key Do's:
- Listen actively and be authentic
- Structure problems systematically
- Demonstrate business judgment
- Think creatively before speaking
- Synthesize findings into conclusions

Key Don'ts:
- Rush analysis without understanding the problem
- Panic if solutions aren't immediately obvious
- Hide your reasoning process
- Use memorized frameworks rigidly
- Rely on advance case knowledge

BCG evaluates "your approach" rather than demanding a single correct answer.
