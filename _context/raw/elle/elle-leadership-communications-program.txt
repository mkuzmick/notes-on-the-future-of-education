Overview
Ineffective communication is a strategic liability that wastes time, stalls decisions, and derails critical initiatives.

The Leadership Communication training program helps you perfect your communication skills, interact with clear intent, establish your confidence and leadership presence, craft and deliver a truly memorable pitch, and master advanced presentation skills.

This executive communication training is designed to be as dynamic as the challenges you face. Through hands-on, real-world learning, you will gain the business communication skills for leaders necessary to overcome public speaking anxiety and develop your personal communication style. Get feedback from expert coaches and practice with AI tools for real-time guidance to boost your confidence while establishing your influence.

 

 Interact with Clear Intent
Understand why effective communication is essential in business and learn how to tailor your communications, whether verbal or nonverbal—from presentations to PowerPoints—to connect with any audience and get the results you want.

 Leverage AI to be a Better Communicator
Refine your executive presence with AI tools that analyze tone, delivery, and message in real-time. Practice confidently, receive objective feedback, and ensure your communication lands with precision with AI communication training.

 Develop Your Confidence and Leadership Presence
Sharpen your verbal and non-verbal communication skills by using your breath, body, and voice to maximize your impact during public speaking. Through practice and coaching, learn to integrate each communication element into your daily habits.

 Perfect Your Personal Communication Style
Define, develop, and refine strategies uniquely suited to you and your business to create memorable pitches, maximize your presence in virtual settings, recognize when something isn’t working, and make masterful adjustments.

 More about the Leadership Communications program
No matter your industry or role, success relies on your ability to communicate effectively. Our executive leadership communication training is part business communications intensive, part interpersonal communications class, part AI-powered communication coaching. This program was designed to help leaders and professionals like you adeptly communicate in high-change business environments and strengthen your impact.

Enjoy An Engaging Program Experience
Throughout the program, faculty directors Adam Leipzig, Penny Kreitzer, and Juliana Schroeder lead participants through a dynamic blend of activities, including: 

- lively discussions and group exercises
- small group coaching and peer-led feedback
- AI tool training and practice
- powerful gestures and body language workshop
- guided practice and rehearsal components
- pitch refinement
- illuminating hands-on exercises
- persuasion goal-setting*
*Note: You will choose a real-world goal and be led through a framework to help articulate, refine, and organize your persuasive strategy.

AI-Powered Feedback
Get guidance on and practice using AI tools for feedback on your leadership communication skills. Keep building on your classroom learnings after the program by continuing to leverage AI at work to refine how you connect with audiences.

Emerge Confident in Your Leadership Communication
Leadership Communications is a communication training for executives that engages you on a profoundly personal leadership development journey to grow your advanced communication skills. You’ll learn how to establish winning communication strategies, create memorable pitches, confidently make adjustments in real-time, be in the moment, and connect to your audience with an authentic, clear, and powerful message.

 How do I enroll in the Leadership Communications program?
Enrolling in any one of our programs is simple:

- Click on the "enroll" button on the program page
- Create an account or log in to our customer portal
- Complete the online reservation form (should take less than 2 minutes)
- Pay by credit card to complete your enrollment, or select your preferred payment type to reserve your seat and connect with the finance team

 How much time can I expect to spend on coursework each day?
We know that time away from the office is precious, so we work hard to make our program experiences as productive and valuable as possible. Our programs span full business days starting between 8:30-9:00 a.m. and running through 5 p.m. with lunch served in the classroom and short breaks throughout the day.

Approximately 1-2 hours of evening activities and preparation for the next day are required for some programs. Please download the program guide for more detail.

 Do your programs provide credits towards a degree, grades, transcripts, or Continuing Education Units (CEUs)?
Our programs are non-degree programs and do not hold any credit unit equivalency, letter grades, pass/fail designation, Continuing Education Units (CEUs), or transcripts. Rather, we provide a certificate of completion at the conclusion of the program.

To qualify for a certificate of completion, a participant must meet the minimum requirements as noted for each program and maintaining at least 80% attendance/engagement.

 Can I enroll in a program if I live outside of the U.S.?
Yes. Enrollment is available to global leaders, but visa requirements may apply. Proficiency in written and spoken English at a level that allows full engagement and participation in a program is recommended.

For most international participants, a B1/B2 visa—for participation in scientific, educational, professional, or business conventions, conferences, or seminars—is required for entry into the United States to attend a program with us.

Requesting and receiving a visa takes time and we recommend beginning the process three or four months in advance of the start date of a program.

You can ask your Program Manager to prepare an invitation letter to help with your visa application. It is important to emphasize that the visit is for business purposes.

The Visa Waiver Program is available to citizens or nationals from select countries. Check the Department of State website to determine your eligibility and learn about the waiver process.

The list of countries sanctioned by the Treasury Department's Office of Foreign Asset Control currently includes: Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria and the Ukraine-Crimea Region, plus the addition of Russia for Berkeley Executive Education at this time.

 How does AI coaching work in the program?
AI tools are used throughout the program to provide real-time, objective feedback on your tone, delivery, and message. You'll learn to leverage these tools at work, continuously strengthening your ability to connect with audiences long after the program ends.
