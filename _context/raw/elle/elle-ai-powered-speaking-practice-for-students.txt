Speakable: AI-Powered Speaking Practice Platform

Speakable is an educational platform that uses artificial intelligence to help students practice speaking and writing skills. Teachers can assign activities that provide instant feedback without requiring manual grading of every response.

Key Features:
- Immediate, rubric-based feedback on student responses
- An AI activity builder for creating custom assignments
- Student portfolios tracking progress over time
- Team collaboration tools for shared rubrics and activities
- Integration with Google Classroom and Canvas
- Support for multiple languages and proficiency levels

Target Users:
The service is designed for language teachers and world language departments, though it also supports English learners and content instruction.

Content Types:
Available activities include role-plays, writing tasks, fluency sprints, cultural comparisons, test prep exercises, and checkpoint assessments.

Teacher Benefits:
Educators praise features like "instant feedback that is possible with the Speakable prompts," time savings on grading, and the ability to see measurable student progress.

Security & Compliance:
The platform includes K-12-ready privacy protections and complies with COPPA, FERPA, and SOPPA regulations.
