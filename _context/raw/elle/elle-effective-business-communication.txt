Effective Business Communication — Chicago Booth Executive Education

Chicago Booth offers an online leadership program focused on enhancing interpersonal communication skills and executive presence. The course teaches professionals how to "motivate and persuade others" through structured frameworks and practical application.

Format & Duration: Six-week online program with live sessions and self-paced modules, requiring 5-6 hours weekly commitment.

Learning Objectives:
The program develops participants' ability to "persuade people by using frameworks that will make your communication engaging, memorable, and compelling." Additional skills include strengthening executive presence, structuring powerful arguments, and improving public speaking capabilities.

Content Areas:
- Persuasion and audience analysis
- Narrative structure and storytelling
- Personal branding
- Habit formation and sustained practice
- Opening and closing techniques

Target Audience: Mid- to senior-level leaders from any functional area seeking to elevate communication effectiveness.

Faculty: Led by Hal Weitzman, Adjunct Professor of Behavioral Science.
